Common questions

What people ask before they begin.

I'm not in Toronto. Can I still work with you?
Yes. All three Designer Room Kits and all three Virtual Interior Design services are available anywhere in the world. The in-person services are limited to Midtown Toronto and surrounding neighbourhoods.
Where should I start if I'm not sure which service is right for me?
If you have one room that is bothering you and want to handle it yourself, start with a Designer Room Kit at $49. If you need someone to tell you specifically what to do in your space, book the $297 Design Direction Session — it includes a written action plan. If you want a complete room or home designed and sourced for you, the Virtual Room Design Experience or Whole Home Design System is the right path.
How is this different from hiring any other interior designer?
Most designers hand over deliverables and disappear. Every service here includes follow-through — a written plan you can act on, a contractor brief that eliminates miscommunication, and direct access to me after delivery so the vision doesn't die between the document and the finished room.
Do I need a large budget to implement what you design?
No. Every sourced shopping list includes a range of price points and a prioritised order of what to invest in first. A room that feels complete and intentional is not the same as a room that is expensive. That difference is what good design is for.
What if I'm not happy with the result?
For all virtual and in-person services, I work with you and revise at no extra charge until you love the result. My commitment is that you feel a real difference — not just satisfied, but genuinely proud of your space. The Room Kits are DIY downloads without revision support, which is reflected in the price point. For a fully supported experience with my revision promise, the virtual and in-person services are the right choice.
