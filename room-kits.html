Room Kit FAQ

Questions about the kits.

What format do I receive?
A high-quality PDF download. You receive it immediately after purchase. Every page is designed to be easy to follow — the shopping list has active links, the paint palette has exact codes, and the implementation guide reads like clear instructions, not design theory.
What if my room dimensions are different from the kit?
Every kit includes two furniture arrangement options to accommodate different room sizes. The paint palette and shopping list apply regardless of dimensions. If your space is unusual or you want guidance on adapting the layout, a $297 Design Direction Session is the right next step.
Can I mix kits from different rooms?
Yes. Each kit is designed with a consistent style language — warm, timeless, character-driven. They are complementary by design. Living Room + Primary Bedroom kits from the same collection will feel cohesive in the same home.
What if I want something more personalised?
That is what the Virtual Interior Design services are for. The Design Direction Session ($297) gives you a personalised plan for your specific space. The Virtual Room Design Experience ($1,497) is a complete custom design for one room. The kits are for people who want professional results on a DIY budget. The virtual services are for people who want the design done for them.
