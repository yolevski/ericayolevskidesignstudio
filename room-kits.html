Is this for you?

This is for you if any of this sounds familiar:

✓ Perfect if...

  • You want a professionally designed room without the full-service price tag
  • You enjoy doing it yourself but want a clear, tested roadmap
  • You're tired of Pinterest boards and want actual decisions made
  • You've been paralysed for months and need a starting point
  • You want to test my style before investing in a full service

✕ Skip if...

  • You want the design customised specifically to your room's dimensions
  • You want a designer to manage contractors for you
  • You need revisions or direct designer access
  • You have an unusual or architecturally complex space
  • You want someone else to execute the room physically

If this applies — check out Virtual Design or In-Person Design instead.
