Virtual Design FAQ

Questions about virtual design.

How does virtual design actually work?
You share photos of your space, dimensions, a completed intake questionnaire, and your goals. I design based on these inputs and deliver a complete digital package — mood boards, floor plans, shopping lists, paint palettes, and contractor briefs. Everything you need to execute it yourself. We communicate via video call and email throughout.
Can virtual design really work for my space?
Yes. I've completed hundreds of rooms virtually. The key is thorough documentation at the intake stage — good photos, accurate dimensions, and a clear brief. I've developed an intake process that captures everything I need to make the right decisions without standing in the room. For spaces with genuinely unusual conditions — challenging natural light, irregular architecture — the in-person services may be a better fit.
What if I start with the Direction Session and then want the full Room Design?
The full $297 you paid for the Direction Session is credited toward the Virtual Room Design Experience or the Whole Home Design System. You never pay twice for the same work.
How long does each service take?
The Direction Session written plan arrives within 48 hours of your call. The Virtual Room Design Experience is delivered within 10 business days. The Whole Home Design System is typically delivered within 14 to 21 business days depending on the number of rooms in scope.
What happens after I receive my design?
For the Room Design, you have a 30-day email follow-up window for questions. For the Whole Home System, you have three 90-day check-in sessions and a 30-day sourcing hotline. I stay involved through the execution phase — revising, advising, and following up until the result matches the vision.
